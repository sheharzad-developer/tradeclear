# TradeClear — Creative Concept & Repositioning

**The shift:** stop selling *compliance* (a chore, a cost center). Start selling *money saved*.

> **TradeClear doesn't help you stay compliant. It helps you pay less duty —
> and we only win when you do.**

Money-first, not compliance-first. That single reframe — not UI, not AI — is what makes
this stand out.

---

## New positioning

> **The duty-savings platform for cross-border DTC brands.**
> Find what you overpaid · pay less on what's next · never get surprised by a tariff again.

Old identity: "AI Trade Compliance Copilot" (generic, commodity, boring).
New identity: a platform whose entire job is **putting duty money back in your pocket.**

## Who (niche-first)

**Fast-growing DTC / Shopify brands shipping cross-border (US ⇄ Canada).**
- Feel tariff pain acutely and immediately
- Ignored by legacy trade software (SAP/Descartes are built for enterprises)
- Reachable through communities, agencies, and ecosystem partners
- Founder-led → fast decisions, no procurement maze

Own this niche completely before going broad.

## The platform = one funnel, not four products

| Stage | What it does | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| 🎣 **LAND** — Found-money refunds | "We find duty you already overpaid and claim it back — we only get paid a % of what you recover." | Risk-free. Proves value in literal dollars. Impossible to say no to. |
| 📈 **EXPAND** — Sourcing & landed-cost engine | "Source here not there, claim this FTA, lower your landed cost on what's next." | Turns a one-time win into ongoing savings. |
| 🔄 **RETAIN** — Tariff watch & simulator | "We watch every tariff change and tell you your move." | The recurring subscription — the reason they never cancel. |

A sharp niche, a money-back hook to land them, ongoing savings to grow them, and
monitoring to keep them. That's a business, not a feature list.

## The demo narrative (lead with the money)

Open on the punchline, not the tool:

> **"We analyzed Acme's catalog and found $14,200 in recoverable duty + $9,800/yr in
> tariff exposure."**

Then let them explore *how*. The hero is a number with a dollar sign — everything else
is supporting evidence.

## What we build / validate FIRST

**Found-money refunds, for the niche.** One thing.
- Customer shares their products / past entries → we surface overpayments → they recover money.
- Success-fee pricing (% of recovery) = the easiest yes in business.
- This earns the account and the data to sell Expand + Retain later.

Tell the story of all three stages. **Build one.**

## Why this isn't a rebuild

The engine already computes overpayment, duty, FTA, and tariff impact. This is **~80%
repositioning** — new identity, niche focus, money-first copy, a savings-led demo — on
the product you already have. "Premature" dissolves by changing what the product *says it
does*, not by adding code.

## What still does NOT change

- Decision-support framing / broker review / importer-of-record liability stays intact.
- Still validate with real customers before building Expand + Retain.
- Discipline holds: **story of three stages, build one wedge.**
