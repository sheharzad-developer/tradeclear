# TradeClear — Outreach Kit (MVP validation)

Goal: land **1–2 real importers** who hand over real SKUs and react to a free analysis.
Validate demand *before* building more. Measure behavior, not compliments.

---

## 1 · One-page pitch (the narrative)

**For** small & mid-size importers into the US and Canada
**who** lose money to HS misclassification, overpaid duties, and missed free-trade-agreement savings,
**TradeClear** is an AI trade-compliance copilot
**that** classifies your products, estimates US + Canada duty, and flags where you may be over- or under-paying — in minutes, from a simple spreadsheet.
**Unlike** waiting on your broker or guessing, it gives you a cited second opinion you can act on.

**Why it matters (the pain in dollars):**
- A single misclassified HS code can mean months of overpaid duty — or penalties for underpayment.
- Most importers have *no independent visibility* into whether their codes are right.
- FTA (USMCA/CUSMA) savings are routinely left on the table.

**What you get in the demo:** upload a product list → get suggested HS codes (with reasoning + sources), US/CA duty estimates, FTA flags, and a "this code looks off" alert per item.

> Decision-support for your customs broker to review — not a customs ruling. You stay in control; we just surface what's worth a second look.

---

## 2 · The offer that gets a "yes": free SKU analysis

Don't sell a demo. Offer **found money**:

> "Send me 20–50 of your real products (just a description + current HS code if you have it) and I'll send back a free report showing the suggested classification, US/Canada duty, and any items where you may be over- or under-paying. No cost, no commitment."

Why it works: it's tangible, low-effort for them, and it tests the only thing that matters — *will they share data and act on the result?*

## 4 · Who to target (build a list of 15–20)

- **SMB / mid-market importers** who self-file or feel under-served by their broker — the sweet spot.
- Roles: owner/founder (small co.), Head of Ops / Supply Chain, Import/Compliance Manager, Logistics lead.
- Sourcing: your own network first → LinkedIn search by title + "import" → trade associations → import-record tools (ImportGenius / Panjiva style).
- **Pick by access first, then by duty pain.** A warm intro in furniture beats a cold list in apparel.

---

## 5 · Discovery-call guide (15 min, mostly listening)

Goal: confirm the pain is real and quantifiable. Talk 20%, listen 80%.

1. "Walk me through how you classify products and file duties today — who does it?"
2. "Has a wrong HS code ever cost you — a penalty, a hold, overpaid duty?" *(listen for $ and stories)*
3. "How confident are you that your current codes are right? How would you even know?"
4. "Do you claim USMCA/CUSMA where you could? How do you decide eligibility?"
5. "If a tool flagged items you're likely over- or under-paying on, what would you do with that?"
6. *(after showing the report)* "What's your reaction? What's missing for this to be useful?"
7. "Would you want this run across your whole catalog? What about your past entries?"

## 6 · Signal scorecard (decide go / no-go)

| Signal | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 Shares real SKUs without much friction | Trust + relevance |
| 🟢 Forwards your report to their broker | They take it seriously |
| 🟢 Asks "can you do all our products / past entries?" | Pull, not push |
| 🟢 Asks about price / timeline | Buying intent |
| 🟡 "Nice tool" but no action | Weak — dig for the real blocker |
| 🔴 Won't share any SKUs | Trust/value gap — fix before building more |

**Validated** = 1–2 importers showing multiple 🟢 signals. That's your green light to invest in real LLM + retrieval over the full HTS, and the v2 overpayment finder.

---

## 7 · Before you demo — non-negotiables

- [ ] **Fractional licensed customs broker (CHB)** on call — validates the duty rates and gives you domain credibility you don't have in-house.
- [ ] Confirm the handful of duty rates in your demo vertical are real (current dataset rates are illustrative).
- [ ] Have 2–3 polished sample reports ready (run public catalogs in your target vertical).
- [ ] Keep the liability framing visible: decision-support, broker reviews, importer of record is responsible.
